Fear not, you are safe.


Hey there, good to know that you are safe. However, as of now, there is no vaccination or cure for covid-19.Yet, you can protect yourself from this deadly virus in the following ways:
  • Wash your hands frequently with a alcohol based sanitizer or hand wash.
  • Maintain social distancing
  • Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth
  • Practice respiratory hygiene
  • Avoid raw food like salads
  • Stay indoors. In case you have to go out, put on a mask. After returning home, make sure that wash you hands throughly and sanitize your belongings

Covid-19 web tracker will help you check how far you are from the nearest Corona positive person. In case, you are within 1-1.5km we advise you to stay extra careful and take utmost precautions.Click here for the COVID19 India Tracker Web App
